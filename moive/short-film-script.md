# Short Film Script

## Title

**Rara-chan's Tiny Luck Delivery**

## One-Line Pitch

A handmade panda doll wakes up, finds a little LazyingArt glow, and delivers it to the viewer like a tiny lucky charm.

## Duration

`18 seconds`

## Storyboard

| Time | Shot | Visual | Motion | Audio |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0:00-0:03 | Wake | Rara-chan sits on a warm cream studio table, eyes catching a soft morning glint. | Slow push-in, tiny head tilt, ears bounce subtly. | One soft bell, light room tone. |
| 0:03-0:06 | Discover | A small golden dot of light appears near the LazyingArt color palette, reflected in the panda eyes. | Panda leans forward with curious charm. | Playful xylophone notes. |
| 0:06-0:10 | Mission | Rara-chan waddles across the table holding the little light like a gift. | Small steps, plush body squash-and-stretch, no cartoon outline. | Gentle beat begins. |
| 0:10-0:14 | Gift | The panda places the glowing dot beside a tiny handmade tag; the glow becomes a warm heart-shaped sparkle. | Camera circles slightly, product remains sharp. | Tiny sparkle sound. |
| 0:14-0:18 | End Card | Clean product pose with LazyingArt logo above or beside the figure. | Slow settle, soft shadow, premium stillness. | Music resolves. |

## Voiceover

Keep voiceover optional. The movie works better as music-first, with only captions at the end.

If voiceover is needed:

```text
Meet Rara-chan, a tiny handmade panda carrying a little luck from LazyingArt.
```
